MATERI 1

Part of Speech (Kata Kelas)

Mengenal 8 jenis kata dalam bahasa Inggris secara detail

📚 Pengertian Part of Speech

Part of Speech adalah klasifikasi kata berdasarkan fungsi dan perannya dalam kalimat. Menguasai ini adalah FONDASI UTAMA bahasa Inggris karena setiap kata memiliki aturan penggunaan yang berbeda.

1. Noun (Kata Benda)

Menyebut orang, tempat, benda, atau konsep.

  • • Concrete: book, table, Jakarta
  • • Abstract: love, happiness, idea
  • • Countable: books, chairs (bisa jamak)
  • • Uncountable: water, information
Contoh: The knowledge is power.
2. Pronoun (Kata Ganti)

Menggantikan noun untuk menghindari pengulangan.

  • • Personal: I, you, he, she, it, we, they
  • • Possessive: mine, yours, his, hers
  • • Demonstrative: this, that, these, those
  • • Relative: who, which, that, whom
Contoh: She loves her book.
3. Verb (Kata Kerja)

Menunjukkan aksi, keadaan, atau proses.

  • • Action: run, think, write
  • • Stative: know, believe, love (tidak progresif)
  • • Transitive: need object (buy, eat)
  • • Intransitive: no object (sleep, arrive)
Contoh: They are studying English.
4. Adjective (Kata Sifat)

Menerangkan noun/pronoun.

  • • Descriptive: beautiful, tall, expensive
  • • Quantitative: many, few, several
  • • Demonstrative: this, that, these
  • • Possessive: my, your, their
Contoh: A beautiful young girl.
5. Adverb (Kata Keterangan)

Menerangkan verb, adjective, atau adverb lain.

  • • Manner: quickly, carefully, well
  • • Time: now, yesterday, soon
  • • Place: here, there, everywhere
  • • Degree: very, extremely, quite
Contoh: She runs very quickly.
6. Preposition (Kata Depan)

Menunjukkan hubungan antara kata.

  • • Time: at, on, in, during, for
  • • Place: at, on, in, under, between
  • • Direction: to, into, through, across
  • • Agent: by (written by him)
Contoh: The book is on the table.
7. Conjunction (Kata Sambung)

Menghubungkan kata, frasa, atau klausa.

  • • Coordinating: FANBOYS (for, and, nor, but, or, yet, so)
  • • Subordinating: because, although, if, when
  • • Correlative: either...or, neither...nor
Contoh: I like tea but she likes coffee.
8. Interjection (Kata Seru)

Ungkapan emosi yang berdiri sendiri.

  • • Joy: Wow! Hurray! Yay!
  • • Surprise: Oh! Ah! Gosh!
  • • Pain: Ouch! Ow!
  • • Greeting: Hello! Hi! Hey!
Contoh: Wow! That's amazing!
💡 Tips Identifikasi Cepat:
  • • Tanya "apa/ siapa?" → Noun
  • • Tanya "melakukan apa?" → Verb
  • • Tanya "seperti apa?" → Adjective
  • • Tanya "bagaimana/ kapan/ di mana?" → Adverb
  • • Ada setelah noun untuk kepemilikan → Preposition

MATERI 2

Personal Pronoun

Mengapa harus menguasai kata ganti person?

⚠️ Mengapa WAJIB Menguasai Personal Pronoun?

Personal pronoun adalah JANTUNG komunikasi bahasa Inggris. Tanpa ini, Anda tidak bisa membuat kalimat yang efisien dan natural. Setiap kalimat bahasa Inggris minimal mengandung 1 pronoun!

Subjective Case

Sebagai SUBJEK kalimat

ISaya
YouKamu/Anda
HeDia (L)
SheDia (P)
ItDia (B)
WeKami/kita
TheyMereka
Contoh: She is a doctor.
Objective Case

Sebagai OBJEK kalimat

MeSaya
YouKamu
HimDia (L)
HerDia (P)
ItDia (B)
UsKami
ThemMereka
Contoh: I love him.
Possessive Case

Menunjukkan kepemilikan

My/MineSaya
Your/YoursKamu
HisDia (L)
Her/HersDia (P)
ItsDia (B)
Our/OursKami
Their/TheirsMereka
Contoh: This is my book. / The book is mine.
🚨 Common Mistakes:
  • ❌ Me and John went there.
  • ✅ John and I went there.
  • ❌ She loves he.
  • ✅ She loves him.
  • ❌ This is her book vs hers book.
  • ✅ This is her book (adj) / The book is hers (pronoun).
🎯 Tips Penggunaan:
  • • It untuk cuaca, waktu, jarak, dan binatang (kecuali peliharaan)
  • • They bisa digunakan singular untuk gender netral: "Someone left their book."
  • • Setelah preposisi (in, on, at, with, etc.) selalu pakai objective case
  • • Possessive adjective (my, your, her) harus diikuti noun
  • • Possessive pronoun (mine, yours, hers) berdiri sendiri

MATERI 3

Subject & Predicate

Struktur dasar kalimat bahasa Inggris

SUBJECT
+
PREDICATE
=
SENTENCE

📍 Subject (Subjek)

Orang, benda, atau konsep yang melakukan aksi atau menjadi topik pembicaraan.

Simple Subject:

Kata benda/pronoun utama

The tall boy runs fast. → Boy

Complete Subject:

Seluruh bagian sebelum verb

The tall boy in blue shirt runs.

Compound Subject:

Lebih dari satu subjek

John and Mary are coming.

🎯 Predicate (Predikat)

Bagian kalimat yang menerangkan apa yang dilakukan subjek.

Simple Predicate:

Verb utama saja

The boy runs very fast.

Complete Predicate:

Verb + objek/keterangan

The boy runs very fast in the morning.

Compound Predicate:

Lebih dari satu verb

She sings and dances.

🔍 Cara Identifikasi Subject:
  1. Tanyakan "Siapa/Apa yang...?" sebelum verb
  2. Abaikan prepositional phrases (in, on, at, etc.)
  3. Subject selalu NOUN atau PRONOUN
  4. Jika ada "there is/are", subject ada sesudah verb

Contoh analisis:

[The students in the classroom] are studying English grammar diligently.

Complete Subject | Complete Predicate

Inverted Order

Verb sebelum subject (pertanyaan)

Are you ready?

Imperative

Subject "you" tersembunyi

[You] Close the door!

Expletive

There/It sebagai pembuka

There is a book on the table.

MATERI 4

Phrase vs Clause

Memahami perbedaan fundamental struktur kalimat

PHRASE

Kelompok kata yang TIDAK mengandung subject dan verb yang saling berhubungan.

Tidak bisa berdiri sendiri sebagai kalimat!

Noun Phrase:

The big red car is fast.

Verb Phrase:

She has been working hard.

Prepositional:

The book is on the table.

Infinitive:

I want to eat now.

Gerund:

Swimming is fun.

Participial:

Running fast, he won.

CLAUSE

Kelompok kata yang MENGANDUNG subject dan verb yang saling berhubungan.

Bisa independent (kalimat) atau dependent!

Independent:

I love you. (Bisa jadi kalimat)

Dependent (Adj):

The man who wears hat is my uncle.

Dependent (Adv):

When I was young, I lived there.

Dependent (Noun):

I know that he is right.

Relative:

The book which I bought is good.

⚡ Perbedaan Kunci:

Phrase:

  • • No subject-verb relationship
  • • Tidak lengkap
  • • Harus digabung dengan clause
  • • Contoh: "in the morning", "running fast"

Clause:

  • • Ada subject + verb
  • • Lengkap tapi bisa dependent
  • • Independent = kalimat
  • • Contoh: "when I wake up", "she sings"

MATERI 5

Verbal & Nominal

Memahami fungsi kata kerja dan kata benda dalam kalimat

🔄 Verbal (Kata Kerja)

Kata yang menunjukkan aksi, keadaan, atau kejadian. Verbal adalah jantung predikat.

Finite Verb

Verb yang punya subject dan menunjukkan waktu (tense).

She writes a letter.

They went home.

Non-finite Verb

Verb yang tidak menunjukkan waktu/subject.

Gerund: Swimming is good.

Infinitive: I want to go.

Participle: Broken heart.

Auxiliary Verb

Helping verb yang membentuk tenses/modal.

Be: am, is, are, was, were

Have: have, has, had

Do: do, does, did

Modals: can, will, must, etc.

🏛️ Nominal (Kata Benda)

Kata atau kelompok kata yang berfungsi sebagai noun (subjek, objek, komplemen).

Jenis Nominal:
  • • Noun: book, happiness, Jakarta
  • • Pronoun: he, she, it, they
  • • Noun Phrase: the big red car
  • • Gerund: Swimming is fun.
  • • Infinitive: To err is human.
  • • Noun Clause: What he said is true.
Fungsi Nominal:
  • • Subject: The dog barks.
  • • Object: I love you.
  • • Complement: She is a doctor.
  • • Object of Prep: Give it to him.
  • • Appositive: My friend, John, is here.
⚖️ Perbedaan Verbal dan Nominal:

Verbal Sentence:

Fokus pada AKSI/KEJADIAN

The cat sleeps on the sofa.

Nominal Sentence:

Fokus pada KEADAAN/IDENTITAS

The cat is an animal.

MATERI 6

V1, V2, V3 (Verb Forms)

Memahami bentuk kata kerja dan penggunaannya

V1 (Base Form)

Bentuk dasar/dictionary

Simple Present:

I go to school.

Infinitive:

I want to go.

Imperative:

Go away!

Modal:

I can go.

V2 (Past Tense)

Simple Past (aktif)

Regular:

walk → walked

play → played

Irregular:

go → went

eat → ate

see → saw

Penggunaan:

I went to Bali yesterday.

V3 (Past Participle)

Perfect & Passive

Regular:

walk → walked

watch → watched

Irregular:

go → gone

eat → eaten

see → seen

Perfect:

I have gone.

Passive:

The cake was eaten.

📋 Tabel Irregular Verbs Populer:

V1 → V2 → V3

see → saw → seen

take → took → taken

V1 → V2 → V3

write → wrote → written

V1 → V2 → V3

begin → began → begun

drink → drank → drunk

swim → swam → swum

sing → sang → sung

V1 = V2 = V3

cut → cut → cut

put → put → put

shut → shut → shut

hurt → hurt → hurt

⚠️ Perbedaan V2 dan V3:

V2 (Past Simple):

  • • Tunggal, tidak perlu auxiliary
  • • Waktu lampau spesifik
  • • Aktif voice
  • • Contoh: She wrote a letter.

V3 (Past Participle):

  • • Selalu butuh auxiliary verb
  • • Perfect tenses & Passive voice
  • • Sebagai adjective
  • • Contoh: She has written a letter.

MATERI 7

Active & Passive Voice

Mengubah fokus pelaku dalam kalimat

ACTIVE VOICE

Subjek MELAKUKAN aksi (fokus pada pelaku).

Formula: S + V + O

The chef cooks the meal.

Si chef memasak makanan.

She wrote a novel.

Dia menulis novel.

They are building a house.

Mereka sedang membangun rumah.

Gunakan ketika: Fokus pada pelaku, informasi langsung, perintah.

PASSIVE VOICE

Subjek DILAKUKI aksi (fokus pada objek/penerima).

Formula: S + to be + V3 + (by agent)

The meal is cooked by the chef.

Makanan dimasak oleh chef.

A novel was written by her.

Novel ditulis olehnya.

A house is being built by them.

Rumah sedang dibangun oleh mereka.

Gunakan ketika: Fokus pada hasil, pelaku tidak penting/unknown, formal writing.
🔄 Rumus Perubahan Active → Passive:
Active:
Subject (doer) + Verb + Object (receiver)
↓
Passive:
Object (receiver) + to be + V3 + by + Subject (doer)

Simple Present:

She eats apple.

Apple is eaten by her.

Present Continuous:

She is eating apple.

Apple is being eaten by her.

Simple Past:

She ate apple.

Apple was eaten by her.

Present Perfect:

She has eaten apple.

Apple has been eaten by her.

⚠️ Catatan Penting:
  • • Hanya transitive verbs (punya objek) yang bisa diubah passive
  • • "By + agent" bisa dihilangkan jika pelaku tidak penting
  • • Modal verbs: can be V3, must be V3, should be V3
  • • Intransitive verbs (sleep, arrive, happen) tidak bisa passive!

MATERI 8

Helping Verb (Auxiliary)

Kata kerja pembantu yang membentuk tenses dan modals

Primary Auxiliaries (Be, Have, Do)

BE (am/is/are/was/were/been/being)
  • • Continuous: is playing
  • • Passive: was eaten
  • • Adjective: is happy
  • • Location: are here
HAVE (have/has/had)
  • • Perfect: have gone
  • • Perfect Cont: has been playing
  • • Possession: have a car
  • • Causative: have it done
DO (do/does/did)
  • • Negatif: do not go
  • • Interogatif: Do you...?
  • • Emphasis: I do love you!
  • • Avoid rept: I like it, so do I

Modal Auxiliaries (Modals)

Tidak berubah bentuk (no -s, -ing, -ed) diikuti V1.

Ability

can, could

I can swim.

Permission

may, can, could

You may go.

Obligation

must, have to, should

You must study.

Possibility

may, might, could

It may rain.

Prediction

will, shall

It will happen.

Advice

should, ought to

You should rest.

Intention

will, would

I will help.

Habit

would, used to

I would play.

🔍 Cara Identifikasi Helping Verb:
  1. Jika ada dua verb atau lebih, yang pertama adalah helping verb
  2. Helping verb selalu diikuti main verb (kecuali short answer)
  3. Modal selalu diikuti V1 (base form)
  4. Have diikuti V3, Be diikuti V-ing atau V3
is + playing
has + eaten
does + not go
can + swim

MATERI 9

Cara Mudah Memahami Tenses

Formula dan pola waktu yang sistematis

🧠 RUMUS MASTER TENSES

Tenses = Waktu (Present/Past/Future) + Aspek (Simple/Continuous/Perfect/Perfect Cont)

SIMPLE

Fakta, kebiasaan, rutinitas

Simple Present:

S + V1/V1+s

I eat. She eats.

Simple Past:

S + V2

I ate.

Simple Future:

S + will + V1

I will eat.

CONTINUOUS

Sedang berlangsung

Present Cont:

S + am/is/are + V-ing

I am eating.

Past Cont:

S + was/were + V-ing

I was eating.

Future Cont:

S + will be + V-ing

I will be eating.

PERFECT

Sudah selesai

Present Perfect:

S + have/has + V3

I have eaten.

Past Perfect:

S + had + V3

I had eaten.

Future Perfect:

S + will have + V3

I will have eaten.

Perfect Continuous (Sedang + Sudah lama)
Present Perf Cont:

S + have/has been + V-ing

I have been eating (for 2 hours).

Past Perf Cont:

S + had been + V-ing

I had been eating (before you came).

Future Perf Cont:

S + will have been + V-ing

I will have been eating (for 2 hours by then).

📊 Time Signals:
  • Simple Present: every day, usually, often, always
  • Present Cont: now, right now, at the moment
  • Simple Past: yesterday, last week, ago, in 1990
  • Present Perfect: already, yet, since, for, ever, never
  • Past Perfect: before, after, when, by the time
  • Future: tomorrow, next week, soon, later
🎯 Tips Menghafal:
  1. Hafalkan pola: S + (aux) + (aux2) + Verb
  2. Simple = V1/V2/will+V1
  3. Continuous = be + V-ing
  4. Perfect = have + V3
  5. Gabungkan: Perfect Continuous = have been + V-ing
  6. Perhatikan time signals dalam soal!

MATERI 10

Conditional, Subjunctive, Inverted

Struktur kalimat kompleks tingkat lanjut

🎲 Conditional Sentences (If Clause)

Type 0 (General Truth)

If + S + V1, S + V1

If you heat ice, it melts.

Type 1 (Real Future)

If + S + V1, S + will V1

If it rains, I will stay.

Type 2 (Unreal Now)

If + S + V2, S + would V1

If I were rich, I would buy it.

Type 3 (Unreal Past)

If + S + had V3, S + would have V3

If I had known, I would have come.

💭 Subjunctive Mood

Mengungkapkan harapan, saran, pentingnya, atau situasi tidak nyata. Menggunakan V1 (base form) untuk semua subjek!

After "That" (Sugest, Demand, etc)
  • I suggest that he study harder. (bukan studies)
  • It is important that she be here. (bukan is)
  • The doctor ordered that I take medicine.

Kata kunci: suggest, demand, require, important, essential, necessary, order, propose

Wish / If Only / As If
  • I wish I were rich. (now - Type 2)
  • I wish I had studied. (past - Type 3)
  • He acts as if he knew everything.
  • If only I were taller!

🔄 Inverted Sentence (Subject-Verb Balik)

Verb/auxiliary diletakkan sebelum subject untuk emphasis atau formal writing.

Negative Adverbs di Awal

Never, seldom, rarely, hardly, barely, no sooner, scarcely

  • Never have I seen such beauty.
  • Seldom does he come late.
  • Hardly had I arrived when it rained.
  • No sooner had I left than he called.
Conditional Inversion

Tanpa "if", menggunakan "were, had, should"

  • Were I rich, I would buy it. (= If I were...)
  • Had I known, I would have come. (= If I had...)
  • Should you see him, tell him. (= If you should...)
Lainnya:

Here comes the bus. (Here/There + verb + S)

Only then did I understand. (Only + time expression)

Not only is he smart, but also kind. (Not only... but also)

Such was the weather that we stayed. (Such + be + S)

⚠️ Catatan Penting:
  • • Subjunctive menggunakan V1 walaupun subject he/she/it (he go, she be)
  • • Inversion selalu menggunakan auxiliary verb untuk simple tenses
  • • "Were" digunakan untuk semua subjek dalam subjunctive (If I were, If he were)
  • • Type 3 Conditional bisa diinvers: Had I known = If I had known
